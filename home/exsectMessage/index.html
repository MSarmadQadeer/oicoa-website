  2. MESSAGE OF EXECUTIVE SECRETARY - OICOA

Our journey, rich with lessons from our religion, the Holy Quran, and Sunnah of our beloved Prophet, Hazrat Muhammad (PBUH) propels us into a future where unity and understanding are paramount among Islamic nations. The principles that have shaped our history—accountability, equity, and moral integrity—remain our guiding stars. As we navigate the complexities of our modern world, these timeless values light our path.
The spirit of collaboration among Ombuds offices is not just a cornerstone of OICOA; it's the heartbeat of our shared mission. Together, we are more than just a collection of nations; we are a symphony of voices, harmonizing to create a world where justice prevails and every individual is valued.
To all the torchbearers of justice, equity, and transparency, your unwavering commitment is the cornerstone of our collective aspirations. As we chart the course of our shared destiny, let each step be a testament to the power of unity and the promise of a world where every voice is not only heard but cherished. Together, let's sculpt a future that generations to come will look upon with pride and gratitude.

Almas Ali Jovindah

Executive Secretary – OICOA
