OIC Ombudsman Association (OICOA)

Rooted in Islamic Ethos - Pioneering Global Accountability and Embracing the Mohtasib Legacy for a Just and Harmonious Tomorrow

OUR MISSION

At OICOA, we are deeply rooted in the ethical principles of Islam, drawing inspiration from the legacy of the Mohtasib. Our mission is to uphold the highest standards of accountability, justice, and principled governance. We envision a harmonious global community, anchored in transparency and the rule of law. Through the power of knowledge and collaborative endeavors, we are steadfast in our commitment to fortify Ombudsman institutions in the modern-day landscape, ensuring that our actions are always guided by the timeless values of justice and righteousness.

MESSAGE OF PRESIDENT - OICOA

From the illustrious capital of the Republic of Türkiye, Ankara, I convey my most profound salutations. It is my distinct honor to extend heartfelt regards to the venerable Ombudsmen of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation Ombudsman Association (OICOA), as well as to the legion of esteemed Ombudsmen who serve with unwavering dedication across our shared global landscape.

MESSAGE OF SECRETARY GENERAL - OICOA

Dr. Asif Mahmood Jah

(Hon’ble Federal Tax Ombudsman, Pakistan)
Secretary General OICOA
Accountability and principled governance stand as paramount tenets in the ethical fabric outlined by Islam. At its core, the Quran-Al-Majeed seeks to establish a societal framework grounded in justice, equity, and moral integrity. Those in positions of authority are particularly urged to eschew dishonesty and injustice. In light of this, Islam introduces the role of the Mohtasib, a precursor from which the foundational principles of every Ombudsman institution within OICOA are drawn.

MESSAGE OF EXECUTIVE SECRETARY – OICOA

Our journey, rich with lessons from our religion, the Holy Quran, and Sunnah of our beloved Prophet, Hazrat Muhammad (PBUH) propels us into a future where unity and understanding are paramount among Islamic nations. The principles that have shaped our history—accountability, equity, and moral integrity—remain our guiding stars. As we navigate the complexities of our modern world, these timeless values light our path.
